Best for

Why it helps

Research strengthens scientific reasoning, communication, persistence, and your ability to connect coursework with real questions.

Simple next steps

  1. Review UB undergraduate research guidance.
  2. Identify departments and faculty whose work interests you.
  3. Prepare a resume and concise outreach email.
  4. Track replies and follow up professionally.

Application reminder

Keep a copy of your resume ready, write a short professional message, track dates and contacts, and confirm everything through the official website before applying.
