Best for

Buffalo-area students interested in hospital volunteering, patient-facing service, and exposure to a major teaching hospital environment.

Why it helps

Volunteering at a large medical center can build professionalism, communication skills, service orientation, and practical understanding of healthcare systems.

Simple next steps

  1. Open the official ECMC volunteer page.
  2. Review age, health, background, and time commitment requirements.
  3. Prepare your contact information and availability.
  4. Apply or contact ECMC Volunteer Services through the official page.

Application reminder

Keep a copy of your resume ready, write a short professional message, track dates and contacts, and confirm everything through the official website before applying.
