Research helps students build scientific thinking, faculty connections, evidence-based reasoning, and a stronger academic story.

Simple next steps

  1. Review faculty research interests.
  2. Choose labs that genuinely match your curiosity.
  3. Email faculty or lab coordinators with a polished resume.
  4. Ask about volunteer, credit-bearing, or assistant roles.

Application reminder

Keep a copy of your resume ready, write a short professional message, track dates and contacts, and confirm everything through the official website before applying.
