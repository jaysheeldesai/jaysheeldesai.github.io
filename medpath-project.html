Why I Built This

A bridge between ambition and direction.

Many pre-med students are motivated, hardworking, and capable, but they often do not know where to begin. They hear about shadowing, research, volunteering, MCAT preparation, leadership, clinical experience, and medical school applications, but the path can feel scattered and overwhelming.

MedPath Connect was created as a project to organize that journey in a clearer and more human way. The goal is to help students understand what steps matter, where to look for opportunities, and how mentorship can make the process easier to navigate.

This project reflects my own growth as a biomedical sciences student, tutor, teaching assistant, research assistant, and future physician. It combines my interest in medicine, education, service, and student support.

Aim & Vision

Helping students move forward with clarity and confidence.

Aim

To create a simple, welcoming, and useful platform where pre-med students can learn about the path to medicine, discover meaningful opportunities, and feel supported instead of lost.

Vision

To become a trusted student-built hub for mentorship, academic resources, clinical exposure guidance, research preparation, and community connection for future healthcare professionals.

Purpose

To make the journey toward medicine more accessible by giving students practical information, encouragement, and a clearer sense of what they can do next.

Founder Note

Built from my own pre-med journey.

I know how overwhelming the path to medicine can feel. As an international student from India studying Biomedical Sciences at the University at Buffalo, I have learned how important guidance, support, and access can be.

Through tutoring, teaching, research, clinical shadowing, and mentorship, I realized that students do not only need information. They need direction, confidence, and someone who understands the journey. MedPath Connect is my way of turning that experience into something helpful for others.
